How To Play❔❔

👉This game requires 2 players.
👉when you roll the dice a random numbr between 1-6 will be rolled.
👉Each roll adds to your current score.
👉 But if you roll 1 your current score will be set to 0 💥💣💥.
👉So, If you click on hold button your current score will be saved to your main score.
👉First player to reach 30 will win the game🥇.

👉👉Play Smart!!👈👈
👉👉Good Luck!!!🙏👈👈
